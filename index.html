About

Welcome! I am a sedimentary geochemist and paleoclimatologist. I research modern and ancient environmental change on land, primarily through the lens of stable isotopes in sediments. I am currently fascinated by soils because they make up the physical interface between climates and landscapes. Download my CV here.
